Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit PDFs online without losing quality?

Yes, modern online PDF editors use advanced algorithms to maintain quality. However, for critical documents, always keep backups of original files.

Are online PDF tools secure for confidential documents?

Reputable tools use client-side processing or secure servers with encryption. Check the tool's privacy policy and avoid uploading highly sensitive documents to unfamiliar services.

What PDF compression level should I use?

Use high compression for web sharing, medium for email, and low or no compression for printing or archiving. Test different settings to find the best balance for your needs.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to editable text?

Yes, using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) tools. Modern OCR can handle various fonts, languages, and even handwriting with high accuracy.

How do I merge PDFs with different page sizes?

Most PDF mergers can handle mixed page sizes. Some tools offer options to standardize page sizes or maintain original dimensions for each page.
